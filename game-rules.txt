Block Blast! Rules (Free Mode)
Drag and Drop Blocks

Place the given block pieces on the board to create and clear full rows or columns.
Clear Lines to Score Points

Whenever you complete a row or column, it disappears, earning you points.
No Rotations

You cannot rotate blocks; you must place them as they appear.
Limited Space

The game ends when you can no longer place any of the available blocks.
No Time Limits

In free mode, you can play at your own pace without time restrictions.
Combo & Streak Bonuses

Clearing multiple lines at once or in consecutive moves grants extra points.
